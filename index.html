It's located in Republic of DRC
in the northeast of the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), approximately 220 kilometers east of capital of the Haut Uele provice,

OWNERSHIP

The mine is owned by the KIBALI GOLDMINES SA(Kibali) which is a joint venture
company effective owned 45% by each of Barrick and AngloGold Ashanti, and 10% by societe miniere de Kilo-Moto (SOKIMO).
The mine is operated by Barrick.
